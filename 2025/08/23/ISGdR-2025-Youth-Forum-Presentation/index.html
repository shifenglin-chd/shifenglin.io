🎤 Conference Overview

I presented my research at the Youth Forum of the 23rd International Symposium on Geo-disaster Reduction (ISGdR 2025) held in Yibin, Sichuan, China.


International Consortium on Geo-disaster Reduction (ICGdR)

📋 Presentation Details

Item Information
Conference 23rd International Symposium on Geo-disaster Reduction (ISGdR 2025)
Event 2025 Youth Forum
Date August 23–26, 2025
Location Yibin, Sichuan, China
Host Southwest Jiaotong University
Presentation Title Influence of Erodible Substrate Topography and Material Strength on Landslide Mobility: Insight from MPM

🔬 Research Highlights

  • Investigated the influence of erodible substrate topography on landslide mobility
  • Analyzed the effect of material strength using Material Point Method (MPM)
  • Explored entrainment mechanisms in landslide dynamics
  • Provided insights for landslide runout prediction and hazard assessment
